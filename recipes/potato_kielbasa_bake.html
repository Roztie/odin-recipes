Potato Kielbasa Bake

Description

He ordered his regular breakfast. Two eggs sunnyside up, hash browns, and two strips of bacon. He continued to look at the menu wondering if this would be the day he added something new. This was also part of the routine. A few seconds of hesitation to see if something else would be added to the order before demuring and saying that would be all. It was the same exact meal that he had ordered every day for the past two years.

Ingriedents

  • 1 small head of cabbage
  • 2 cups of diced potatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of smoked kielbasa sausage, sliced
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 slices bread, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup of butter, sliced

Steps

  1. Prehead oven to 400 degees
  2. Remove and wash outer leaves of cabbage
  3. Line bottom of a 13x9 pan with cabbage leaves
  4. Shred remaing cabbage
  5. Mix shreeded cabbage, potatoes, and smoked sausage in a bowl; spread over the cabbage leaves in the baking dish
  6. Whisk milk, eggs, salt and pepper together in a bowl, add bread cubes. Set aside until bread absorbs some liquid(1 to 2 minutes)
  7. Pour over the cabbage mixture. Top with butter slices
  8. Bake in preheated oven until potatos are tender abd the top is lightly browned about 45 minutes
